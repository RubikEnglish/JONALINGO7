"The Emperor and the Kite"

"El Emperador y la Cometa"

71. At the palace, the emperor was greeted by wild and cheering crowds.



72. The people were tired of the evil men, but they had been afraid to act.



73. With the emperor once again to guide them, they threw the plotters into prison.



74. And when the other sons and daughters of the emperor heard of his return, they left off their sobbing and sighing, and they hurried home to welcome their father.



75.But when they arrived, they were surprised to find Djeow Seow on a tiny throne by their father’s side.



76. To the end of his days, the emperor ruled with princess Djeow Seow close by. She never wanted for anything, but love.



77. And the emperor never again neglected a person- whether great or small.



78. And, too, it is said that Djeow Seow ruled after him, like the wind, at once gentle, but in his loyalty, inflexible.



The end
